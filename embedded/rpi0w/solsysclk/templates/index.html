GPS Coordinates

Please enter the GPS coordinates of your Solar System Clock in decimal degrees:

Latitude (N/S):

Enter positive number in decimal degrees for N (0 to 90), negative number for S (-90 to 0)

Longitude (W/E):

Enter positive number for E (0 to 180), negative number for W (-180 to 0)

For example:
Lat: 38.9072, Lon: -77.0369 are the coordinates for Washington, DC.
Lat: -33.8688, Lon: 151.2093 are the coordinates for Sydney, Australia.
